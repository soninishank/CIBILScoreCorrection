## Myth 1: Checking my own CIBIL score reduces it  
**False.** Only lender checks reduce your score.

## Myth 2: Settled loans disappear after few months  
**False.** Settlement stays for **7 years**.

## Myth 3: Paying minimum due is safe  
**False.** It creates debt trap and high utilization.

## Myth 4: CIBIL score improves automatically  
It improves only with **action**.

## Myth 5: Closing a credit card increases score  
Often, it **reduces** score.

## Myth 6: All bureaus have the same score  
Each bureau calculates differently.

## Myth 7: Paying EMI early increases score  
It does nothing.

## Myth 8: CIBIL has power to change my score  
Only banks/NBFCs can.

## Myth 9: Score depends on income  
Your **repayment history**, not income, matters.

## Myth 10: Credit repair agencies can magically fix everything  
Credit repair requires documentation and lender updates.
